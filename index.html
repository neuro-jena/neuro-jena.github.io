This is the website of the Structural Brain Mapping Group at the Departments of Psychiatry and Neurology of the Jena University Hospital. Our principal research focuses on the development of methods for structural brain imaging and their application. Specific areas of interest include the investigation of ageing, structural brain plasticity, and schizophrenia research.
